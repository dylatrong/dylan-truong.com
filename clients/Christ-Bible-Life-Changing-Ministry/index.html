Our Mission

The mission of Christ Bible Life Changing Ministry is to equip individuals with a clear understanding of who they are, restore hope to those who feel lost or forgotten, and demonstrate love through consistent service to the community.

We are committed to serving the whole person. This includes addressing spiritual needs while also responding to practical challenges such as hunger, unemployment, lack of education, addiction, and poverty.

Through compassionate outreach, teaching, and hands-on support, the ministry helps individuals gain the confidence, knowledge, and tools needed to improve their circumstances and make positive, lasting changes in their lives.
